Web 3.0

Web 3.0, also known as Web3, is the third generation of the World Wide Web. Web 3.0 is meant to be decentralized, open to everyone (with a bottom-up design), and built on top of blockchain technologies and developments in the Semantic Web, which describes the web as a network of meaningfully linked data.

Blockchain

A blockchain is a digital ledger that records transactions across multiple computers. It's a decentralized, distributed, and often public digital ledger.A blockchain is made up of records called blocks. These blocks are used to record transactions across many computers. The blocks are often public, which means that anyone can participate in the network without restrictions.

Generative AI

Generative AI, or generative artificial intelligence, is a type of AI that can generate new content like text, images, music, audio, and videos. Generative AI uses generative models to generate data, often in response to prompts. These models learn the structure and patterns of their input data and then generate new data with similar characteristics.
